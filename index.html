The Journey

Cracking It, One Egg at a Time

Jogeshwari East's favourite street-side egg stall. Fresh, protein-packed egg dishes made hot on the tawa, right in front of you. Built on hygiene, consistency and local trust.

Founder Mohammad Irfan built this stall the old-school way, one perfectly made egg dish at a time. What began as a small roadside stand in Jogeshwari (East) grew purely through word-of-mouth: garment hub workers, students and daily commuters who came once and kept coming back.

The formula has never changed: consistency, hygiene and trust. Every egg is cracked fresh, every plate is served hot, and every customer is treated like a regular.

"Simple food, made honestly, served fast. That's it. That's the whole secret."
